Our Products

Premium quality construction materials for all your building and infrastructure needs

River Sand

Ideal for plastering and masonry work. Natural river sand with excellent binding properties and smooth finish.

Usage: Plastering, Masonry, Concrete

M-Sand (Manufactured Sand)

Best for RCC and concrete applications. Consistent quality with superior strength and durability.

Usage: RCC, Concrete, High-strength Applications

Aggregates (10mm / 20mm / 40mm)

High-strength construction aggregates available in multiple sizes for various concrete applications.

Usage: Concrete, RCC, Structural Work

Stone Chips

Clean and durable crushed stone with excellent compressive strength for heavy-duty construction.

Usage: Foundation, Road Base, Drainage

GSB & Filling Material

For road and infrastructure projects. High-quality granular sub-base material meeting all specifications.

Usage: Road Construction, Infrastructure, Backfilling
